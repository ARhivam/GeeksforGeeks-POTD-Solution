About This Scraper

What it can scrape:

  • Headings (e.g., <h1>, <h2>, <h3> tags) from the specified webpage.
  • Images (e.g., <img> tags, including SVGs) from the specified webpage.

What it cannot scrape:

  • Dynamic content generated by JavaScript (unless specifically handled).
  • Content from websites with strict scraping policies (robots.txt restrictions).
  • Content behind paywalls or requiring user authentication.
  • Data that requires complex navigation or interaction (like forms).

Use this tool responsibly and in accordance with the terms of service of the websites you are scraping.
